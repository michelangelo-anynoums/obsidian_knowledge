# 30-Day Wi-Fi Cybersecurity Roadmap

## Study Strategy

Each day:

- 45–90 minutes
    
- Read theory (30 min)
    
- Watch demonstrations (15–30 min)
    
- Take notes (15 min)
    
- Draw diagrams from memory
    

Goal:  
By Day 30, understand Wi-Fi well enough to analyze captures, understand WPA2/WPA3, identify wireless attacks, and communicate with network engineers.
