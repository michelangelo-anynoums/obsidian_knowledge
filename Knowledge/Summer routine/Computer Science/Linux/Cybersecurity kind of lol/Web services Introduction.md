### **What is ==Cybersecurity==?**

Cybersecurity refers to the practice of ==protecting systems, networks, and data== from digital threats, such as hacking, malware, phishing, and data breaches. 

With the increasing reliance on technology and the internet, ==cybersecurity is crucial for safeguarding personal information, sensitive business data, and critical infrastructure.== It involves a variety of practices, tools, and measures aimed at preventing unauthorized access, attacks, or damage to digital assets. 

As cyber threats continue to evolve, staying informed and using effective security tools ==is essential for both individuals and organizations.==

### **Useful web services**

### 1. **VirusTotal**

**Website**: [virustotal.com](https://www.virustotal.com)  
**Description**: Analyzes files and URLs using multiple antivirus engines to detect malware.

### 2. **ScamAdviser**

**Website**: [scamadviser.com](https://www.scamadviser.com)  
**Description**: Rates websites for safety based on domain info, user reviews, and red flags.

### 3. **Hybrid Analysis**

**Website**: [hybrid-analysis.com](https://www.hybrid-analysis.com)  
**Description**: Provides deep behavioral analysis of files and URLs to detect threats.

### 4. **Have I Been Pwned**

**Website**: [haveibeenpwned.com](https://www.haveibeenpwned.com)  
**Description**: Checks if your email has been part of a data breach.

### 5. **Shodan**

**Website**: [shodan.io](https://www.shodan.io)  
**Description**: A search engine for discovering exposed internet-connected devices.

### 6. **URLVoid**

**Website**: [urlvoid.com](https://www.urlvoid.com)  
**Description**: Scans URLs for reputation and checks if they're blacklisted or dangerous.

### 7. **Cuckoo Sandbox**

**Website**: [cuckoosandbox.org](https://cuckoosandbox.org)  
**Description**: Analyzes files in a sandbox environment to detect malicious behavior.

### 8. **WOT (Web of Trust)**

**Website**: [mywot.com](https://mywot.com)
**Description**: Provides website safety ratings based on crowdsourced data.
